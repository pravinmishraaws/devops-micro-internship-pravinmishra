# Reflection – Week 1

### Biggest insight I got about myself this week

I almost didn't post this because Week 1 didn't feel like 'progress'.
I spent most of my time setting up environments, watching tutorials, and reading docs — and barely any time actually building. It felt productive in the moment. It wasn't.

The pattern I noticed: I use "learning more" as a way to avoid the discomfort of just trying something and getting it wrong.

New rule starting this week: terminal open first, tutorial second. 20 minutes of just attempting the task before I let myself search for help.
5 months of DMI ahead. This is week 1 of the honest version.

### My biggest weakness/loop I noticed

I tend to research/watch tutorials longer than necessary before actually building anything, which delays hands-on practice and makes progress feel slower than it is.

### One system I will implement from this week (exact habit + time)

Every morning, before opening any tutorial or docs, I open my terminal first and attempt the task hands-on for 20 minutes — only searching for help when I actually get stuck.
